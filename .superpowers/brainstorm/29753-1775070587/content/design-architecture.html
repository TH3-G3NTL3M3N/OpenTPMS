Key Architecture Decisions

Two-Block Symmetric

Electronics and battery separated by valve hole. Same injection mold for both enclosures. Battery's metal can kept away from antenna. Balanced weight on wheel.

Pre-Certified Module

Raytac MDBT42Q handles nRF52832 + antenna + crystal + matching network. FCC/CE/IC pre-certified. Our PCB is a simple 2-layer carrier board.

Replaceable CR1225

48mAh coin cell in spring-clip holder. User swaps during sealant refresh (every 1-2 years). Screw-down lid + O-ring seal on battery block.

Supercap Cold Buffer

47-100mF supercapacitor absorbs TX current bursts. Enables -30В°C operation from a CR1225. Charges during sleep from trickle current.
