Tire Insert Compatibility Analysis

Can our sensor fit alongside popular tire inserts? All inserts have a valve stem cutout — our sensor lives there.

Insert Cross-Section Profiles vs Our Sensor

All inserts use a T-shape or teacup profile. The narrow base sits in the rim bed, the wider top supports the sidewall. The valve stem area always has a cutout or channel.

CushCore Pro (MTB)

Cross section:55mm wide x 23mm tall
Base width on rim bed:~20-25mm (narrow base)
Rim bed contact height:~8-10mm at narrowest
Valve cutout:Uses proprietary valve with cross-drilled hole. Insert presses against valve area.
Rim width:22-35mm internal

⚠ TIGHT FIT — CushCore sits directly on valve stem base. Our sensor height is critical here.

CushCore Trail / XC

Cross section:55mm wide x 19mm tall (Trail) / smaller (XC)
Base on rim bed:~18-22mm
Rim bed height:~6-8mm at base
Valve cutout:Same proprietary valve system
Rim width:22-35mm (Trail), 22-32mm (XC)

⚠ MODERATE — shorter profile gives more clearance

Tannus Armour Tubeless

Foam thickness:14-20mm compressed
Design:Wings sit against/above tire bead
Valve area:Open channel — doesn't obstruct valve. Works with standard valves.
Rim width:19mm+ internal

✓ GOOD FIT — open valve channel means plenty of clearance

Vittoria Air-Liner

Design:Lightweight foam tube
Valve area:Uses side-entry "Multiway" valve. Insert has channel for valve.
Rim width:Various sizes

✓ GOOD FIT — valve channel design leaves space

Our Sensor Height Budget

CR1225 battery height: 2.5mm
PCB thickness (4-layer FR4): 0.8mm (using thin PCB)
Components on PCB (nRF module, sensor, etc): ~1.5mm tallest component
Enclosure walls + O-ring + screw heads: ~1.5mm (top + bottom shell)
Supercapacitor: ~1.5mm
TOTAL estimated height: ~5.5 - 6.5mm

Verdict

Tannus & Vittoria: Compatible — open valve channels leave plenty of room for a 5.5-6.5mm sensor.

CushCore Trail/XC: Likely compatible — 6-8mm base height with valve cutout should clear our sensor, but needs physical testing during prototyping.

CushCore Pro: Tight / may require trimming — 8-10mm base sits very close to valve stem. Our 5.5-6.5mm sensor might fit in the valve cutout but it's borderline. CushCore Pro users already use proprietary valves, so some accommodation is expected.

Recommendation: Target ≤5.5mm total height. This clears all inserts except possibly CushCore Pro, which we'll verify during prototyping and document compatibility.
