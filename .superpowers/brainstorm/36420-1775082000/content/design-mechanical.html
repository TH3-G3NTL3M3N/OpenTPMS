Block B is 0.4mm over target. Mitigation options:
  • Use PCB cutout under battery — CR1225 sits in a routed pocket, PCB only around the edges for traces. Saves 0.4-0.6mm.
  • Or use direct spring contacts soldered to PCB instead of a holder — eliminates the 0.5mm clip height. Battery sits directly on PCB pads.
  • Or accept 5.9mm — still compatible with all inserts except possibly CushCore Pro.

Sealing Design

O-Ring Groove

Rectangular groove machined/printed into the body rim. Silicone O-ring (1mm cross-section) sits in groove. Lid compresses O-ring ~25% when screwed down. Standard static seal design per AS568 guidelines.

Screw Retention

2x M1.2 stainless Allen screws per block. Thread into brass heat-set inserts pressed into ASA body. Loctite 222 (low-strength threadlocker) prevents vibration loosening. Included Allen key in box.

ePTFE Pressure Port (Block B lid)

2mm hole in Block B lid. ePTFE membrane bonded on inner surface with medical-grade adhesive. Air passes through for pressure reading. Sealant, water, and debris blocked. Inspectable during battery swap.

Conformal Coating

Exposed PCB strip between blocks (valve area) coated with silicone conformal coat. Protects traces from sealant and moisture. Applied during assembly.

3D Printing Specs (ASA)

MaterialASA (Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate) — UV and chemical resistant
Layer height0.1mm (fine detail for O-ring groove)
Infill100% — structural, waterproof walls
Post-processingAcetone vapor smoothing on O-ring surfaces for gas-tight seal
Brass insertsM1.2 heat-set threaded inserts, pressed in with soldering iron tip
Tolerances±0.1mm achievable at 0.1mm layer height on a well-calibrated printer
