Critical Test Procedures

1. Pressure Accuracy

Method: Compare sensor reading against a calibrated digital gauge (Topeak SmartGauge D2 or similar, ±0.5% reference) at 10 PSI increments from 0-125 PSI.

Pass: All readings within ±0.5% of reference across full range.

Temperature sweep: Repeat at -30°C (freezer), 20°C (room), 60°C (oven). Verify temp compensation algorithm correctness.

2. Seal Integrity

Method: Submerge assembled sensor in water at 1m depth for 30 minutes. Inspect for moisture ingress (moisture-indicating paper inside enclosure).

Pass: Zero moisture detected (IP67 equivalent).

Sealant test: Install in tire with sealant, ride 100km. Remove and inspect — no sealant inside enclosure, ePTFE membrane clear, pressure readings unchanged.

3. RF Range

Method: Sensor installed in tire on aluminum and carbon rims. Garmin head unit on handlebars. Ride and monitor for dropouts.

Pass: Continuous connection with zero dropouts over 1-hour ride on both rim materials. ANT+ and BLE tested independently.

Range test: Walk away from bike with head unit — should maintain connection to at least 3 meters (typical handlebar-to-wheel distance is <1m).

4. Battery Life Validation

Method: Measure average current with Nordic Power Profiler Kit II during each state: deep sleep, active riding (3s TX), waking. Calculate expected life from 48mAh CR1225.

Pass: Deep sleep <5µA. Average riding current <20µA. Calculated life >2 years at 1hr/day.

Accelerated test: Run sensor continuously for 1 week, measure total drain, extrapolate.

5. Vibration & Shock

Method: Mount on MTB, ride aggressive trails for 20+ hours total. Inspect solder joints, screw retention, O-ring seating, frame-to-PCB bond after.

Pass: No cracked joints, no loose screws, no seal degradation, pressure accuracy unchanged. Continuous data logging during rides shows no anomalies.

6. Flat Detection

Method: Simulate slow leak (loosen valve core slightly while riding). Verify alert triggers within 30 seconds of pressure crossing compensated threshold. Also verify NO false alert during a 20°C temperature drop (ride from sun into shade/evening).

Pass: Real leak detected within 30s. Zero false alerts from temperature changes across 3 test rides.

7. Cold Weather (-30°C)

Method: Place sensor in freezer at -30°C for 2 hours. Remove and immediately test: does it wake on motion? Does it transmit? Is pressure reading accurate? Does supercap deliver TX burst current?

Pass: Successful wake, transmission, and accurate pressure reading within 10 seconds of first motion at -30°C.

8. Insert Compatibility

Method: Install sensor + each tire insert brand on a 25mm internal width rim. Verify tire seats, insert doesn't crush sensor, sensor reads accurately.

Test with: CushCore XC, CushCore Trail, Tannus Tubeless Pro, Vittoria Air-Liner. Document each result.

Pass: Tire seats normally, sensor undamaged, readings accurate for each compatible insert.
